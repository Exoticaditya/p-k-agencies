About P.K. Agencies

P.K. Agencies is a leading manufacturer and wholesale supplier of premium wedding and event furniture. With years of experience in the industry, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in providing high-quality products for weddings, banquets, and special events.

Located at 2257, Sarai Top Khana, H.C. Sen Road, Chandni Chowk, Delhi-6, we serve clients across Delhi and beyond with our extensive range of products including Shamiana, Kanat, Jaimala Chairs, Banquet Chairs, Stainless Steel Sofas, decorative items, and much more.

Our commitment to quality, competitive pricing, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for event planners, decorators, and businesses in the hospitality industry.
